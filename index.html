Computer Security Incident Response Team (CSIRT)

Computer Security Incident Response Team (CSIRT) is a team that provides services in preventing, mitigating and responding to cybersecurity incidents, in a region (constituency) that is responsible for receiving, monitoring and handling reports and activities of cybersecurity incidents held by the government. CSIRT provides services to report incidents or vulnerabilities owned by the government website so that improvements can be made.
